# enchildada red sauce
### ingredients

    3T oil
    3T flour
    1T chili powder
    1t garlic powder
    1/2t cumin
    1/4t oregano
    pinch cinamon
    2T tomato paste
    2C chicken stock
    2T vinegar

### how do

    make a roux with the oil and dry ingredients
    add paste and stock, cook for a few min
    add vinegar
